
Perunthenaruvi Waterfalls are waterfalls 36  km from Pathanamthitta in Pathanamthitta District, Central Travancore region, Kerala State,India. It is a popular tourist destination. It is situated in the Vechoochira Panchayat.
The name "Perunthenaruvi" means "The Great Honey Stream".
Located on the Western Ghats of the Sahyadri Range, Perunthenaruvi is famous for the waterfalls there. The waterfalls are known for their wide area, rather than their height. The stream later unites with the Pamba River. It is beautiful and dangerous at the same time. 
How to Reach
Tourist from Pathanamthitta can reach here by travelling through Ranni, Vechoochira.
From highrange or kottayam, go through Erumely- Mukkoottuthara.
From Perunad: 8 kms
